Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for users worldwide.

Does Zpendr work offline?

Yes. It is local-first and designed to work without constant internet access.

Can I use the web version?

Yes. The web version opens in a new tab for browser use.

Is my data private?

Your data stays on your device by default, with optional sync if you enable it.

Can I switch languages?

Yes. Use the language selector to choose your preferred language.
