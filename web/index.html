Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for users worldwide.

Do I need an account to use Zpendr?

No. You can start tracking expenses, setting budgets, and managing bills the moment you open the app — no signup required. Your data is saved locally on your device automatically.

Creating a free account unlocks the ability to back up your data and restore it if you switch devices. A Pro subscription adds real-time cloud sync across all your devices.

🔒 Local save — free, always ☁️ Cloud sync — Pro
How is my data saved without an account?

All your transactions, budgets, and bill reminders are stored directly on your device using local storage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your data stays completely private and works fully offline.

If you uninstall the app or clear app data, local records will be removed — so exporting a backup beforehand is recommended.

📱 On-device storage 🚫 No server upload 📤 Export anytime
What does cloud sync do on the paid plan?

With a Pro subscription, your data is encrypted and synced securely to the cloud. This means your transactions, budgets, and settings are always up to date across every device you use — phone, tablet, and desktop.

You also get automatic daily backups, so you can restore your full history at any time without manual exports.

🔄 Real-time sync 🔐 Encrypted backups 📲 All devices
How does sync work with a local-first app?

Your default experience stays local — the app works fully without internet. When cloud sync is enabled (Pro), changes made on any device are merged automatically the next time you come online. Local data always takes priority so you never lose offline edits.

Can I export my data anytime?

Yes, on both free and paid plans. Export your full transaction history as a PDF or Excel spreadsheet at any time — useful for tax reporting, sharing with an accountant, or keeping a personal backup outside the app.

What happens if I change phones or laptops?

If you use local storage only, export your data before switching devices. On the Pro plan, just sign in on your new device and your full history syncs automatically — nothing to transfer manually.

🆓 Free — export before switching ⭐ Pro — sign in and sync
Are AI features required?

No. AI tools are optional extras — available on Pro — for spending insights, automatic transaction categorisation, and monthly forecasts. Core budgeting works completely without them.
